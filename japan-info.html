🎌 Essential tips

Cash is king   Carry ¥50,000–100,000 per person. 7-Eleven and Japan Post ATMs reliably accept foreign cards.
Suica IC card   Get at airport on arrival. Works on almost all trains, buses, and convenience stores across the country. Top up at any JR machine.
Luggage forwarding (Takkyubin)   Send bags city-to-city via Yamato Transport (~¥2,000/bag). Next-day delivery. Hotels hold them. Game-changing on travel days.
Convenience stores   7-Eleven, Lawson, FamilyMart are genuinely excellent — fresh food, ATMs, hot meals, printing, everything.
Etiquette   No eating while walking. Quiet on trains. Remove shoes in traditional spaces. Always queue. No tipping — ever.
For Sarah   Accessible stations marked on Google Maps (elevator icon). Carry water, rest often in heat. Keep medical card in Japanese on you at all times.
Weather   Late Sep: 22–27°C. Early Oct: 18–24°C. Comfortable walking weather. Pack a light layer for evenings.
