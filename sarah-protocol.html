Regular Requirements (Detail) ▼

MRI Scans

  • Frequency: Every 6 months (June and December)
  • Location: Royal North Shore Imaging, MRI Department
  • Purpose: Monitor MS progression and treatment effectiveness
  • Booking lead time: Book 2–3 months in advance

JC Virus Blood Test

  • Frequency: Every 6 months (February/March and August/September)
  • Location: Douglas Hanley Moir Pathology
  • Purpose: Monitor risk related to MS treatment
  • Booking lead time: Book 1 month in advance

Full Blood Test

  • Frequency: Annual
  • Location: GP or pathology provider
  • Purpose: General health monitoring
  • Booking lead time: Book 1 month in advance

Neurologist Appointments

  • Frequency: Twice per year (June and December)
  • Location: Suite 405, Level 4 "The William", 480 Pacific Hwy, St Leonards NSW 2065
  • Format: June — Telehealth / December — Face-to-face
  • June appointment: Request driver's licence medical letter
  • Booking lead time: Book 3–6 months in advance

Driver's Licence Medical Letter

  • Frequency: Annual (June neurologist appointment)
  • Purpose: NSW driver's licence medical clearance
  • Action required: Request letter during June telehealth appointment
